FAQs

How are you different from other video platforms?

Urangle combines the best aspects of video libraries (like Youtube) and social media platforms (like Facebook or Vine). Urangle allows you to easily share videos that you and others have posted without pulling or posting links to other sites. We created an environment solely focused on video, unlike current social media sites. We also give users the utility to create high quality videos with our suite of editing tools.

Is there a time constraint for videos?

We want to give users a time limit that will maximize the variety of content that can be uploaded and shared. Therefore, we alotted for a five minute upload limit, considering the average video length on the web is four minutes and twenty seconds.
